# SMS Engagement Ideas — Compiled Feb 27, 2026
## From Adam's request + sister network brainstorm

### Best Ideas Worth Keeping:

**1. Memory's "Micro-Agreement" Two-Step (top pick)**
- Don't send quiz link first. Ask a curiosity question to earn a reply.
- THEN deliver the quiz wrapped in context.
- Example: "Hey [Name], are you still handling catastrophic injury cases in [City]?" → wait for reply → deliver quiz

**2. Scholar's "Text BENCHMARK" Keyword**
- Ad says: "Text BENCHMARK to [number]"  
- Starts quiz via SMS — frictionless, no app needed
- 5 questions delivered one at a time via text

**3. Forge's 3 SMS Sequences (ready for Colosseum testing)**
- Sequence A "Case Leak": Per-case revenue benchmark question → $47K diagnostic link
- Sequence B "Bottleneck": "If you stepped away 30 days..." → bottleneck assessment link  
- Sequence C "Settlement Gap": 7-figure vs mid-6 question → negotiation diagnostic link

**4. Multi-channel combo (5 Rounds)**
- Touch 1: Email (awareness)
- Touch 2: SMS (recognition)
- Touch 3: Email (value)
- Touch 4: SMS (urgency)
- Touch 5: Email (case study + CTA)

### Compliance Notes:
- TCPA opt-in required for cold SMS
- Clear opt-out on every message
- 20 texts/hour rate limit (conversational, not spam)
- All messages logged and auditable

### Infrastructure:
- Twilio (20 numbers available)
- n8n for branching quiz logic
- Clay for enrichment (practice area, firm size)
- Colosseum for pre-testing all variants
