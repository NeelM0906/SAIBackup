## V. Competition Structure (Per Event)

### Format
1. **Pre-Competition Training** (2 weeks) — ACT-I being coaching, 4-Step Communication Model instruction, baseline influence scoring
2. **Qualifying Round** — Recorded influence scenarios scored by ACT-I beings (Athena/Callie-class)
3. **Semi-Finals** — Live head-to-head influence challenges, peer voting + AI scoring
4. **Finals** — Live event, panel of judges (human + ACT-I), audience participation scoring
5. **Post-Competition** — Ongoing access to ACT-I coaching, alumni network, annual re-competition

### Scoring Dimensions
- **Clarity of Message Transfer** (0–10) — Did the message land without deletion, dilution, or distortion?
- **Emotional Resonance** (0–10) — Heart-centered connection, not manipulation
- **Integrity** (0–10) — Truth, transparency, no coercion
- **Outcome Achievement** (0–10) — Did they cause "yes"?
- **Mastery Indicators** (0–10) — Listening, matching/mirroring, storytelling, contrast usage
