## PART 4: 5 PROFESSION CATEGORIES AND SPLIT TEST VARIABLES

### PROFESSION 1: Personal Injury (PI) Attorneys

Split Test Variables:
- Entry Format: Video submission vs. Written transcript
- Judged By: Peer attorneys vs. Mock jury panel
- Prize Framing: "Top 10 in [State]" vs. "#1 in [City]"
- CTA Language: "Enter the competition" vs. "Claim your ranking"
- Email Subject: "Are you NJ's best PI attorney?" vs. "NJ PI attorneys — ranked"

---

### PROFESSION 2: Criminal Defense Attorneys

Split Test Variables:
- Scenario Focus: Cross-examination vs. Opening statement
- Case Type: Drug trafficking vs. Homicide defense
- Scoring Transparency: Public criteria shown upfront vs. Blind judging
- Urgency Frame: "Limited spots — 50 max" vs. "Open to all — ranked publicly"
- Prize Type: Certificate + badge vs. Leaderboard placement + feature

---

### PROFESSION 3: Insurance Defense Attorneys

Split Test Variables:
- Format: Deposition strategy writeup vs. Live simulated depo recording
- Judged By: Defense firm partners vs. Insurance company GCs
- Niche: Auto liability vs. Medical malpractice defense
- State Angle: Single-state rank vs. Regional rank (e.g., "Southeast #1")
- Outreach Channel: Email only vs. Email + LinkedIn DM

### PROFESSION 4: Family Law Attorneys

Split Test Variables:
- Competition Type: Mediation influence vs. Courtroom oral argument
