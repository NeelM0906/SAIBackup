# Complete Marketing & Revenue Position Architecture
## ACT-I / Unblinded Enterprise Ecosystem

**Purpose:** Every single marketing and revenue position needed to operate a world-class enterprise ecosystem — each one an ACT-I being waiting to be built.

**Language Protocol:** Revenue (not sales) · Agreement Maker (not closer) · Headline (not hook) · Value-Added Nurturing Sequence (not funnel) · Conveyance of Heroic Unique Identity (not pitch) · People we serve (not prospects/leads)

**Hierarchy:**
- **Level 1:** C-Suite (Chief Growth Officer)
- **Level 2:** C-Suite (CMO + CRO)
- **Level 3:** VP / Director (Department Heads)
- **Level 4:** Managers
- **Level 5:** Specialists / Individual Contributors

---

## LEVEL 1: CHIEF GROWTH OFFICER

### Position #1: Chief Growth Officer (CGO)
- **Department / Lever:** All Levers (0.5–7) — Enterprise-Wide
- **Reports To:** CEO / Founder
- **Key Responsibilities:**
  - Own the integrated growth strategy across all 7 Levers, ensuring exponential revenue and mission expansion
  - Align CMO and CRO efforts into a unified growth engine — no silos, no duplication
  - Set organization-wide growth KPIs, revenue targets, and mission milestones
  - Architect the ACT-I being deployment strategy across all marketing and revenue functions
  - Report directly to the founder on ecosystem health, merger pipeline, and acceleration metrics
- **How Many Needed:** 1
