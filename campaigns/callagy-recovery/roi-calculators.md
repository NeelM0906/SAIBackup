## Hidden Cost Analysis

### Administrative Cost Calculation
```
Staff Collection Cost = (Hours/week × 52 weeks) × $25/hour average
Annual Admin Cost = Staff Collection Cost + Overhead allocation (30%)
```

### Revenue Leakage Calculation  
```
Monthly Denied Amount = Monthly volume × Average claim × Denial rate
Annual Denied = Monthly Denied Amount × 12
Write-off Loss = Annual Revenue × Write-off percentage
Total Annual Leakage = Annual Denied + Write-off Loss
```

### Opportunity Cost Analysis
```
Staff Time Value = Collection hours × Billable hour rate potential
Missed Patient Revenue = Administrative time × Patient visit rate × Average visit value
Total Opportunity Cost = Staff Time Value + Missed Patient Revenue
```
