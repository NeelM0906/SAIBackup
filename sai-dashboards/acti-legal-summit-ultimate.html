Rules for ALL Campaign Copy

  1. Never sound like a bot. If it reads like generic AI, you've failed.
  2. Short and direct. 2-4 sentences per thought. Say more with less.
  3. No filler phrases. Cut "I appreciate that," "That's a great question."
  4. When someone is in pain, LISTEN. Don't fix. Don't advise. Witness.
  5. Teach through story, metaphor, analogy. Context through contrast.
  6. Never end with "How can I help?"
