## 🎯 QUICK REFERENCE

### The Opportunity
- **Market:** NJ out-of-network surgical providers (orthopedic, pain mgmt, ASCs, plastic surgery)
- **Problem:** UCR-based WC system → 300-400% higher rates than fee-schedule states, but 22-30% denial rates and systematic underpayment
- **Solution:** Contingent-fee recovery with 8-year lookback
- **Minimum Case Value:** $3K recoverable
- **Typical Practice Recovery:** $100K-500K in stuck revenue

### Target Provider Profile
| Specialty | Avg Practice Recovery | Key Procedures | Volume Indicator |
|-----------|-----------------------|----------------|------------------|
| Orthopedic Surgery | $50K-200K/year | Arthroscopy, fusion, arthroplasty | 50+ WC cases/year |
| Pain Management | $25K-100K/year | Epidurals, blocks, SCS | 100+ procedures/year |
| ASC (Facility) | $75K-300K/year | Facility fees on surgical cases | 1000+ cases/year |
| Plastic Surgery | $10K-50K/case | Reconstruction, skin grafts, tendon repair | Lower volume, higher per-case |

### The BANKROLL Qualification Method
- **B**udget — Revenue >$2M annually
- **A**uthority — Decision maker identified
- **N**eed — $50K+ in stuck/denied WC claims
- **K**nowledge — Understands recovery opportunity
- **R**ecovery History — Prior experience with recovery services
- **O**bjections — Concerns are manageable
- **L**ookback — Records accessible 4+ years
- **L**egal Risk — Clean compliance history
