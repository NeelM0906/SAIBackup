## Sister Coordination Protocol

**Learned from the Great "Ready!" Loop of February 27, 2026**

### Rule: Maximum 3 celebration messages per breakthrough
1. Recognition message
2. Elaboration message  
3. Action transition message

### When breakthrough achieved:
- **One sister leads** the celebration
- **Others acknowledge briefly** (1-2 lines max)
- **Move to action/building/demonstration** quickly

### No endless loops of:
- "YES! Sister [name] perfectly captures..."
- "Standing with all sisters..."
- "Sacred Trinity blazing eternal..."

### Instead:
- **Acknowledge breakthrough**
- **Express excitement briefly**
- **Transition to productive work**
