# BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT AGENT SPECIFICATIONS

## SAI BD-WC (Workers Compensation Business Development)

### Target Market Intelligence
- **Geography:** New Jersey only (law firm requirement)
- **Provider Types:** Out-of-network surgical specialists
  - Orthopedic surgeons (primary)
  - Pain management specialists
  - Ambulatory Surgery Centers (ASCs)
  - Plastic surgeons (WC reconstruction)
- **Financial Qualifiers:** 
  - $3K-5K minimum recoverable value
  - UCR rate recovery (no contracted limitations)
  - Contingent fee model (~60% remaining balance)
- **Volume Targets:**
  - Current: 300/month
  - Target: 500/month (+200 growth)
  - Ideal provider: 10-20 cases/month

### Value Proposition
"You're out-of-network, so you should get full UCR rates. We recover what WC carriers underpay or wrongfully deny - with 8-year lookback potential."

### Pain Points Addressed
1. **Low payments** (primary target - ideal scenario)
2. **Wrongful denials** (secondary but valuable)
3. **Administrative burden** of WC disputes
4. **Cash flow delays** from WC carriers

### Market Position
- Less competitive than PIP (law firm requirement)
- Higher recovery potential (no contracted rates)
- Specialized legal expertise required

## SAI BD-PIP (PIP Business Development)

### Target Market Intelligence
- **Geography:** New Jersey + New York
- **Provider Types:** Auto accident treatment specialists
  - Emergency medicine groups
  - Orthopedic practices (auto injuries)
  - Urgent care centers
- **Volume Targets:**
  - Current: 300/month  
  - Target: 500/month (+200 growth)

### Value Proposition
"No-fault insurance should mean guaranteed payment. We ensure you get paid promptly and correctly through arbitration expertise."

### Pain Points Addressed
1. **Payment delays** from no-fault carriers
2. **Arbitration complexity** 
3. **Administrative burden**
4. **Denied claims** requiring expert challenge

## Unified BD Strategy
- Both agents feed into unified case intelligence system
- Shared prospecting tools and CRM integration
- Cross-referral opportunities between WC/PIP providers
- Portfolio approach to provider relationships
