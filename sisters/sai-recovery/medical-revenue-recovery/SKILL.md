# Medical Revenue Recovery

## Overview

This skill provides specialized knowledge for medical revenue recovery operations - helping healthcare providers recover money owed through insurance claims, denied payments, and complex billing scenarios. Covers both technical automation and strategic recovery approaches.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. File Intake & Triage Automation
Automate the intake and initial processing of medical billing files:
- Patient data extraction and validation
- Insurance eligibility verification  
- Prior authorization tracking
- File routing based on payer type and complexity

**Key Scripts:**
- `scripts/file_intake.py` - Automated file processing and data extraction
- `scripts/insurance_discovery.py` - Hidden coverage detection algorithms

### 2. Denial Management & Appeals
Strategic approaches to insurance denials and appeals:
- AI-powered denial prediction and prevention
- Payer-specific appeal strategies
- Documentation requirements by payer type
- Success rate tracking and optimization

**Reference Materials:**
- `references/denial_patterns.md` - Common denial reasons and solutions
- `references/payer_strategies.md` - Payer-specific negotiation approaches

### 3. Third-Tier Legal Escalation
When standard billing and collections fail, escalate to legal action:
- Arbitration preparation and filing
- No Surprises Act compliance
- State-specific billing regulations
- Legal documentation templates

**Assets:**
- `assets/arbitration_templates/` - Legal filing templates
- `assets/compliance_checklists/` - Regulatory compliance guides

### 4. Performance Metrics & KPIs
Track and optimize recovery performance:
- Clean claim rate optimization (target: >90%)
- Denial rate minimization (target: <5%)
- Net collection ratio maximization (target: >95%)
- Aged A/R management (<30% over 90 days)

### 5. Technology Integration
Leverage AI and automation for superhuman performance:
- Predictive file scoring algorithms
- Real-time eligibility verification
- Automated prior authorization workflows
- Insurance discovery technology deployment

## Workflow Decision Tree

**For new files:**
1. **Automated Intake** → Extract data, verify eligibility, route appropriately
2. **Initial Submission** → Clean claims with >90% success rate
3. **Denial Management** → AI-powered appeals with payer-specific strategies
4. **Legal Escalation** → Third-tier billing with arbitration/litigation

**For existing files:**
1. **File Review** → 6-year lookback analysis
2. **Recovery Strategy** → Payer pattern matching
3. **Negotiation** → Relationship-based approach
4. **Resolution** → Settlement or legal action
