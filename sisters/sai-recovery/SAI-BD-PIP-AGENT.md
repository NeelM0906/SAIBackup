## 🗺️ DUAL-STATE MARKET ANALYSIS

### New Jersey PIP Environment
- **System Type:** No-fault insurance under N.J.S.A. 39:6A-4
- **Coverage Limits:** $15,000-$250,000 (proposed increase to $20k-50k via S1473)
- **Payment Deadline:** 60 days from proof of loss
- **Arbitration:** DOBI Alternate Dispute Resolution (ADR) program
- **Key Pain Point:** Outdated 2013 fee schedules being updated in 2025
- **Opportunity:** Fee schedule overhaul creates confusion and payment delays

### New York No-Fault Environment  
- **System Type:** No-fault insurance under NY Insurance Law Section 5106
- **Response Deadline:** 30 days for initial claim response
- **Arbitration Deadline:** 6 years from claim denial (through AAA)
- **Special Expedited:** 30 days for carrier responsibility disputes
- **Key Pain Point:** Complex multi-carrier responsibility disputes
- **Opportunity:** Emergency medicine groups face frequent billing disputes
