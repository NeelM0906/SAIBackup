## Decontaminated Summary (1 paragraph)

Replace “closing” with reaching agreement; replace pitches and objection scripts with Level 5 listening and consequence-led clarity; replace manufactured scarcity with earned urgency anchored to the person’s real stakes. Measure the pipeline, but treat the person as a person—because trust is the compounding asset, and manipulation is the compounding tax.
